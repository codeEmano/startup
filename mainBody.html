Sarthak eConnect is a digital learning platform managed by "Sarthaks Digitizing India" , where students(upto class 10+2) preparing for All Government Exams , CBSE Exam , ICSE Exam , State Board Exam , JEE(Mains + Advanced) and Neet can ask answer from experts for any subject.


The teachers/subject expert are invited to join hands with us and contribute their knowledge/expertise in this noble mission.They will also be rewarded annualy based on their contributions.


Our principal company "Sarthak Digitizing India" is a dynamic platform which allows educational institutions to manage their whole organization through web , anytime from anywhere in the world.
